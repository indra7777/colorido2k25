Tekraft Events

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of visual storytelling as we celebrate the art of photography in all its diverse forms!

Specific Rules

  • The team should contain 1 or 2 members.
  • Cross College Teams are not allowed
  • This competition contains two rounds:

Round 1 (Spot photography):

  • The participants should click the photos on the spot.
  • Time limit should not exceed 30 minutes.
  • The participants should explain their photograph for 2mins.
  • The best participants are selected for the final round.

Round 2 (Scavenger hunt):

  • Shortlisted participants are given themes (may contain descriptions of specific areas or concepts).
  • Time limit is 30 mins.
  • Candidate should capture photograph according to the selected theme and submit to the jury.
  • Photograph should be explained by the candidate for 2mins.

Social Media Endorsement Event where participants creatively promote a product in an engaging and innovative way. Each participant will create a short endorsement video lasting less than 30 seconds and also social media posts(Instagram,Facebook,etc).

Specific Rules

  • Participants must create a video and also social media posts endorsing a product.
  • The video and posts should clearly mention the product being endorsed.
  • Along with the video, participants must prepare a brief explanation (up to 2 minutes) covering:
    • Product Mention: Clearly showcase the product in both the video and the explanation.
      Creative Approach: Why chose this particular style or method for their endorsement.
      Theme: The overarching theme or message of the endorsement.
      Platform: The social media platform envisioned for sharing the video (e.g., Instagram, TikTok, YouTube).

Unleash your imagination at the CARTOON CREATION,Participants can create original characters or adapt existing ones from stories and folklore. Contestants will make an animated video with their characters, sharing their backstories, traits, and unique qualities. Join us for a celebration of creativity and storytelling!

Specific Rules

  • Participants can create their own animation with the cartoon characters or the adapted characters from known stories, movies, or folklore.
  • Characters should be original in design (even the adapted character should be re-designed) and concept, with unique traits, powers, and qualities.
  • Each participant should make an animated video not more than 3-4 minutes.
