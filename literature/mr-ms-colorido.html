Fashion Walk

The Fashion Walk is a lively event where students showcase their creativity, style, and imagination through fashion-themed performances. Organized by the college's cultural committee, it celebrates fashion, art, and self-expression. Teams will design stunning costumes, choreograph their walks, and present innovative styles based on a theme of their choiceâ€”ranging from futuristic to retro, cultural fusion, or any imaginative concept. It offers participants a platform to flaunt their creativity, teamwork, and confidence on the runway.

Performance Rules

  • The fashion walk is open to all students of the college.
  • No cross college teams are allowed.
  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 8 and a maximum of 10 participants.
  • Each team must select a theme for their walk.
  • No participant can be part of more than one team.
  • Each walk must be within 3-4 minutes, including entry and exit.

Theme and Costume Guidelines

  • The chosen theme should be relevant, and costumes must align with it. Creativity and originality are encouraged.
  • Costumes should maintain decency and avoid vulgarity or offensive imagery.
  • No political, religious, or offensive symbols should be used in any form.
  • Teams may use accessories, props, and makeup to enhance the theme presentation. These should not pose a safety risk.

Performance Guidelines

  • Teams must choreograph their own walk, and it should be synchronized with the music.
  • Each team is responsible for providing their background music in a proper format at least 3 days before the event..
  • No pyrotechnics, sharp objects, or hazardous materials are allowed as part of the performance.
  • Team members should not throw objects or make contact with the audience during the performance
  • Teams must not damage any stage property or college facilities.
  • All participants must follow safety protocols. Any actions that pose risks to the participants or audience will result in disqualification.
  • Team members are advised not to wear costumes or props that could cause injury to themselves or others.

    General Instructions

  • All the Participants should carry their college ID Card.
  • Registration must be done before the commencement of the event.
  • Any suspicious doing can lead to direct disqualification.
  • Judges' decision will be final.
  • Any violation of rules leads to negative marking.
  • Every event is strictly time-bound.
  • Cross College Teamms are not allowed.
